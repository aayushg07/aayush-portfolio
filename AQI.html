AQI UI Concept

Project information:

  • Category: UI Design
  • Objective: AQI Navigator
  • Project Description: The project entails developing an Apple Watch and mobile application that offers real-time monitoring of the Air Quality Index (AQI) customized to users' locations. It delivers timely updates on air quality conditions and directs users to nearby safe areas during critical AQI levels. Furthermore, the app provides convenient access to emergency assistance by identifying nearby pharmacies or medical facilities, ensuring swift access to essential healthcare services during emergencies.
  • Technology Used: Figma
  • Role Played: Managed project poster design and crafted high-fidelity and low-fidelity prototypes using Figma for user-centered design.
